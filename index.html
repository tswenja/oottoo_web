關於巫闍 About OottoO

吟誦祝禱，以舞祈福
用聲波為媒介，自身為載體
連結宇宙能量，平衡身體與心靈

世界各國的古文化中，「巫」一向是療癒身心能量的執行者，時以原始吟唱的音律上達天聽連結內心，時而伴隨著手繪的圖紋祝禱祈福，更常在充滿力量的舞蹈中，融合樂聲在節奏中喚醒迷失的靈魂。

「巫闍」的誕生即是源自於這樣的啟發。
每個文化的語言、音樂、舞蹈、紋飾不盡相同，卻都能讓人們在其中找到靈魂連結的樞紐，而這正是巫闍團隊所期盼創造的—— 汲取自古文化裡巫師們的精神，以舞為針，以樂音為線，以裝置藝術為媒材，以山海為祭壇，讓台灣的戶外音樂祭活動用自己的節奏與世界共鳴，也讓每一個參與者和我們在過程中相互療癒，共同交織出更讓人會心流連的奇幻旅程。

Prayers chant, praying with dances
Using sound waves as a medium and oneself as a carrier
Linking cosmic energy, to balance body and soul.

In ancient cultures around the world, "Witch/Shaman/巫” have always been the executors of healing energy for the body and soul. They use the primeval chanting rhythm, hand-painted patterns, accompanied by powerful dances to pray for blessings and connect hearts to reach heaven in trance, fusing music to awaken the lost souls in the rhythm.

“OoTToO" is derived from this inspiration.

The language, music, dance, and ornamentation of each culture are different, but they all allow people to find a hub for soul connection among them, which is precisely what the OoTToO team hopes to create - drawing on the spirit of wizards from the ancient culture, using dance as the needle, music as the string, installation art as the medium, mountains, and seas as the altar, to make Taiwan's outdoor music festival activities can resonate with the world with its rhythm, also allows each participant to heal each other in the process, interweaving a more engaging and magical journey.
